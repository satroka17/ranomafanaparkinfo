About Us

Welcome to Ranomafana!

This website was designed in collaboration with the United States Peace Corps and Madagascar National Parks with you in mind. It is our biggest goal to attract tourists who prefer independent travel, designing their own vacation, and full immersion to this tropical paradise. It is our next biggest goal to help you make the absolute most of your visit once you do arrive!


You’ll find several tabs of information here to make booking your trip to Ranomafana easy and simple. The first is everything you might need to know about hotels, including prices, amenities, and contact information. Most hotels here are not yet online, which can make making a reservation quite tricky as a tourist from abroad. The next tab will include information on Ranomafana National Park, the town’s main attraction. This will help to set you up nicely for all the nature and hikes you hope to do. The next tab provides information on other activities that you can (and should!) participate in around town. This includes waterfall hikes, hot springs, kayaking, and more - be sure to check it out. Next is important information on how exactly to get to town from Antananarivo via multiple forms of transportation. This can be tricky, but this information will set you straight. Finally, we dive into educational information on how your ecotourism supports conservation, provide a donation link to Madagascar National Parks, and share a basic map of Ranomafana town.

We look forward to welcoming you to Ranomafana, and hope to see you soon!
